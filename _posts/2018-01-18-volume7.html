In the world of cryptocurrency there's only two emotions:

  1. FOMO, the Fear Of Missing Out, when the total market value is really high.
  2. Full Panic Mode, when the total market value is really low.

Something you need to remember is that you technically haven't lost money yet until you sell at a loss. That being said, sometimes it's a good idea to cut your losses. For example, on a failed ICO. The moral of the story is to be patient. Interestingly enough, January 15th or 16th is always the worst day of the entire year for crypto.
